## Entity Name

## Entity Type

## Description

The Photographic Records Collection contains photographs, image archives, visual records, event images, personal photographs, organizational photographs, and related visual materials documented throughout the Steve Avrus Archive.

The collection serves as a visual record of people, locations, organizations, activities, gatherings, and events associated with entities documented throughout the repository. Individual images may provide contextual evidence, establish relationships, document participation, preserve historical moments, or support the reconstruction of events and timelines.

As additional materials are identified and preserved, the Photographic Records Collection functions as a central repository for visual records connected to the broader archive and investigation.
