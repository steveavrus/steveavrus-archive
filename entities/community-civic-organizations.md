## Description

Community Civic Organizations represents the collection of civic associations, volunteer organizations, service groups, advisory bodies, neighborhood initiatives, charitable activities, community projects, and public participation organizations referenced throughout the Steve Avrus Archive.

These organizations provide important context regarding civic engagement, public service, leadership participation, organizational influence, and community development activities documented within the repository.

The entity functions as a central organizational registry connecting community records, leadership activities, public events, social networks, and related archive materials. As additional records are identified, the scope of Community Civic Organizations continues to expand through the inclusion of newly discovered organizations and related activities.
