The South Florida Social Club Network represents a collection of social clubs, membership organizations, private gatherings, community groups, lifestyle associations, recreational activities, and related social environments referenced throughout the Steve Avrus Archive.

Records associated with the network may include membership references, event participation, correspondence, photographs, introductions, invitations, and community activities.

As additional records emerge, the South Florida Social Club Network continues to evolve through the identification of new organizations, relationships, events, and social connections documented throughout the archive.
