## Description

The Community Leadership Network represents the collection of civic leaders, organizers, advisors, volunteers, board participants, community advocates, and influential individuals appearing throughout the Steve Avrus Archive.

The network serves as a framework for understanding how leadership roles, public participation, community influence, organizational activity, and civic engagement intersect across multiple entities documented within the repository. Relationships within the network frequently extend across organizations, events, initiatives, and social environments.

As records continue to emerge, the Community Leadership Network provides context for examining how individuals contributed to, influenced, or participated in community activities documented throughout the archive.
